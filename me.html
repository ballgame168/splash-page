Josh Itzoe

401(k) Expert

Joshua P. Itzoe, CFP®, AIF® is a Partner and Managing Director of Greenspring Wealth Management’s Institutional Client Group. An SEC-registered investment adviser, Greenspring provides independent retirement plan consulting services to publicly-traded, privately-held, and non-profit organizations throughout the country. Itzoe is respected as an industry expert, commentator and thought leader on matters pertaining to retirement security, fiduciary responsibility and defined contribution plans.
